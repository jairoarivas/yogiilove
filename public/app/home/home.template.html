YOGA FOR A CAUSE

Sunday yoga classes at Peacock Park were started in hopes to collect enough money to help children in Nicaragua. We weren't sure HOW to help, we just knew we had to help. Slowly, things have been falling into place with a few changes along the way.

All the proceeds go towards a tiny village near the Cosiguina Volcano in Chinandega, Nicaragua.

How much we can help depends on how much money we can collect by the end of the year!

If you can't donate money, don't let that stop you from attending! You can "give" by spreading the word.
