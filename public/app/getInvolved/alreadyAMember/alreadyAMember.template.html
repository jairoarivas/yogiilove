Point System

For Fall and Spring Semester you must obtain a total of 20 points.You may obtain points by:

  • Attending any UF MEDLIFE event
      This could include:
    • Fundraisers
    • Workshops
    • Socials
    • Service
    • Philanthropy
  • Wearing your MEDLIFE shirt when we table (max. 2 points)
  • Bringing a friend to a MEDLIFE event
    • You may obtain a maximum number of 2 points.
    • You may bring the same friend for both points.
  • Participating in a MEDLIFE committee (2 points per committee) (max. 4 points)
  • Mentioning and tagging UF MEDLIFE in a post on Instagram/ Facebook (each post 1 point) (max. 3 points)
      For a post to qualify, you must include:
    • At least one relevant photo
    • A brief description for the photo
    • Tag UF MEDLIFE

You may lose points for failing to attend Mandatory Events.
